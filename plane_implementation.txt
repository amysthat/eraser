
the plane has 6 states:

# patrol:
goes around a specified path

# detect_shock:
activates when the player is in sight and close enough.
after a certain time transitions to "approach".

# approach:
approaches the player until it is close enough, indefinitely.

# charge:
launches towards the player.

# hit:
weakened state. only happens when parried or missed.
upon parry: 1.5 seconds
upon miss: 0.5 seconds

# destroyed:
goes back to start point. waits until the player is out of sight or far enough,
then reconstructs and switches to "patrol".
